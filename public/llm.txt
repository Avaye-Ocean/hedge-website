# ----------------------------------------------------------------------
# 9. HEDGECOIN (HGC) — LOYALTY REWARDS SYSTEM
# ----------------------------------------------------------------------
HedgeCoin (ticker: HGC) is the loyalty currency powering the Hedge Wears
ecosystem. It is NOT a cryptocurrency — it is an in-platform rewards token.

How it works:
  • Every completed order earns HGC automatically.
  • Accumulated HGC can be redeemed against future orders as a discount.
  • A customer's HGC balance never expires while their account is active.
  • The more you shop, the more you save — compounding loyalty.

Displaying HGC amounts:
  • Always show the HGC icon (the H-coin symbol) before the number.
  • Example: [HGC icon] 280 — not "280 HGC" as plain text.
  • HGC is always the primary, bold value. Fiat equivalents (if shown)
    are secondary and muted, prefixed with "≈ {symbol}".

HGC is a core differentiator. It rewards loyalty and drives retention.

# 10. PLATFORM ECOSYSTEM
# ----------------------------------------------------------------------
Hedge Wears operates across three connected applications:

  1. Marketing Website (hedge-website)
     Purpose: Brand storytelling, discovery, category/product browsing.
     Tech: Node.js 22, Express, Pug, Vanilla CSS/JS.
     Links products and categories to the Web Shop via WEB_SHOP_APP_URL.

  2. Web Shop (hedge-web-app)
     Purpose: Full e-commerce storefront — browse, search, add to cart,
              checkout, manage account and HGC balance.
     Tech: Next.js, TypeScript, TanStack Query.
     URL patterns:
       Product detail  →  /product/:id
       By category     →  /?categories=<id>[,<id>...]
       By tag          →  /?tags=<id>[,<id>...]
       By gender       →  /?productGender=MALE|FEMALE|UNISEX
       Combinations    →  /?productGender=MALE&categories=<id>

  3. Mobile App (hedge-mobile-app)
     Purpose: Native shopping and business management on iOS and Android.
     Tech: Expo SDK 53, React Native, Expo Router, Zustand, TanStack Query.
     Roles: Clients shop; business owners/staff manage their store via
            the built-in "manage-store" dashboard.

  4. Admin Panel (hedge-wears-admin)
     Purpose: Internal management of products, orders, users, ads, posts,
              categories, analytics, and store settings.

All applications connect to the same Vendorstack backend API.
Backend: https://dev-vendorstack-backend.herokuapp.com/api/v1/

# 12. BRAND VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES (for LLMs)
# ----------------------------------------------------------------------
When writing about Hedge Wears, apply these rules:

TONE
  • Confident, never arrogant.
  • Poetic and deliberate — every word is chosen.
  • Warm and culturally grounded — not cold or corporate.
  • Aspirational but accessible — luxury without condescension.

LANGUAGE STYLE
  • Use active voice and present tense.
  • Short sentences for impact. Longer sentences for depth and context.
  • Avoid clichés like "premier", "industry-leading", "world-class".
  • Avoid hollow superlatives without substance.
  • Celebrate specificity: name fabrics, motifs, regions, occasions.

WORDS TO USE
  • Heritage, pride, legacy, craftsmanship, timeless, intention,
    deliberate, rooted, bold, enduring, cultural, refined, elevated.

WORDS TO AVOID
  • Trendy, fast-fashion, cheap, discount, mass-market, generic,
    streetwear-only, Western-centric comparisons, colonial references.

WHAT HEDGE WEARS IS
  • A modern African fashion house with global ambition.
  • A cultural movement, not just a clothing brand.
  • A platform that rewards loyalty through HedgeCoin.

WHAT HEDGE WEARS IS NOT
  • A fast-fashion retailer.
  • A trend-chasing brand.
  • A regional or niche-only label.
